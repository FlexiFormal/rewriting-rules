Rule 10.4: Modal "True"-VP Reduction
====================================


```{rewrite-rule}
VP : <VP> = "divide $5$"

x : <Term> = "$x$"

"it @is true | is the case | holds@ that :x: :VP:"

--- Modal "True"-VP Reduction

":x: :VP:"
```


Examples
--------

```{rewrite-rule}
"it is the case that $x$ divides $5$"

--- Modal "True"-VP Reduction

"$x$ divides $5$"
```
