Rule 4.8: "And"-NP Aggregation
==============================


```{rewrite-rule}
$x$ is (a | no) {ph}`NP`$_1$ and ... and $x$ is (a | no) {ph}`NP`$_n$

$x$ is (a | no) {ph}`NP`$_1$ and ... and (a | no) {ph}`NP`$_n$
```

Examples
--------

```{rewrite-rule}
$x$ is a prime number and $x$ no even number

$x$ a prime number and no even number
```
