Rule 11.1: "Is"-NP Double Negation Elimination
==============================================


```{rewrite-rule}
$\vdash \forall \varphi.\, \neg \neg \varphi = \varphi$

$x$ is not no {ph}`NP`

$x$ is a {ph}`NP`
```


Examples
--------

```{rewrite-rule}
$x$ is not no prime number

$x$ is a prime number
```
