Rule 10.7: Modal "True"-AP Reduction
====================================


```{rewrite-rule}
AP : <AP> = "non-empty"

x : <Term> = "$x$"

"it @(is true | is the case | holds)@ that :x: is :AP:"

--- Modal "True"-AP Reduction

":x: is :AP:"
```


Examples
--------

```{rewrite-rule}
"it is the case that $x$ is non-empy"

--- Modal "True"-AP Reduction

"$x$ is non-empty"
```
