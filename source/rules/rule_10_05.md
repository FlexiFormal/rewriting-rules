Rule 10.5: Modal "False"-VP Reduction
=====================================


```{rewrite-rule}
it (is wrong | is false | is not the case | does not hold) that $x$ {ph}`VP`

$x$ does not {ph}`VP`
```


Examples
--------

```{rewrite-rule}
it does not hold that $x$ intersects with $y$

$x$ does not intersect with $y$
```
