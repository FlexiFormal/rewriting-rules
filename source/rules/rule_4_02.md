Rule 4.2: "Or"-AP Aggregation
=============================


```{rewrite-rule}
AP_1, ..., AP_n : <AP> = "prime", "greater than $5$"

t : <Term> = "$x$"

":t: is :AP_1: or ... or :t: is :AP_n:"

--- "Or"-AP Aggregation

":t: is :AP_1: or ... or :AP_n:"
```

Examples
--------

```{rewrite-rule}
"$x$ is prime or $x$ is greater than $5$"

"$x$ is prime or greater than $5$"
```
