Rule 11.10: "Or"-Idempotence Reduction
======================================


```{rewrite-rule}
Prop : <Prop> = "$x$ is prime"

P : <Var> = "$P$"

|- $\forall :P:.\, :P: \vee :P: = :P:$

":Prop: or :Prop:"

--- "Or"-Idempotence Reduction

":Prop:"
```


Examples
--------

```{rewrite-rule}
|- $\forall P.\, P \vee P = P$

"$x$ is prime or $x$ is prime"

"$x$ is prime"
```
