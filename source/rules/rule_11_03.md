Rule 11.3: Adjective Double Negation Elimination
================================================


```{rewrite-rule}
$\vdash \forall \varphi.\, \neg \neg \varphi = \varphi$

$x$ is not non-{ph}`adjective`

$x$ is {ph}`adjective`
```


Examples
--------

```{rewrite-rule}
$x$ is not non-empty

$x$ is empty
```
