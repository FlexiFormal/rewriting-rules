Rule 10.8: Modal "False"-AP Reduction
=====================================


```{rewrite-rule}
AP : <AP> = "prime"

x : <Term> = "$x$"

"it @is wrong | is false | is not the case | does not hold@ that :x: is :AP:"

--- Modal "False"-AP Reduction

":x: is not :AP:"
```


Examples
--------

```{rewrite-rule}
"it does not hold that $n$ is prime"

--- Modal "False"-AP Reduction

"$n$ is not prime"
```
