Rule 11.5: VP Double Negation Elimination
=========================================


```{rewrite-rule}
$\vdash \forall \varphi.\, \neg \neg \varphi = \varphi$

$x$ does not not {ph}`VP`

$x$ {ph}`VP`
```


Examples
--------

```{rewrite-rule}
$x$ does not not intersect with $y$

$x$ intersects with $y$
```
