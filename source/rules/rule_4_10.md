Rule 4.10: "Neither"-AP Aggregation
===================================


```{rewrite-rule}
$x$ is not {ph}`AP`$_1$ and ... and $x$ is not {ph}`AP`$_n$

$x$ is neither {ph}`AP`$_1$ nor ... nor {ph}`AP`$_n$
```

Examples
--------

```{rewrite-rule}
$x$ is not even and $x$ is not prime

$x$ is neither even nor prime
```
