Rule 4.10: "Neither"-AP Aggregation
===================================


```{rewrite-rule}
AP_1, ..., AP_n : <AP> = "even", "prime"

t : <Term> = "$x$"

":t: is not :AP_1: and ... and :t: is not :AP_n:"

":t: is neither :AP_1: nor ... nor :AP_n:"
```

Examples
--------

```{rewrite-rule}
"$x$ is not even and $x$ is not prime"

"$x$ is neither even nor prime"
```
