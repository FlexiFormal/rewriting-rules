Rule 11.11: "And"-Commutativity Conversion
=========================================


```{rewrite-rule}
$\vdash \forall \varphi, \psi.\, \varphi \wedge \psi = \psi \wedge \varphi$

{ph}`PP`$_1$ and {ph}`PP`$_2$

{ph}`PP`$_2$ and {ph}`PP`$_1$
```


Examples
--------

```{rewrite-rule}
$x$ is prime and $x$ is even

$x$ is even and $x$ is prime
```
