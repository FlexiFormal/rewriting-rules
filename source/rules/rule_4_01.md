Rule 4.1: "And"-AP Aggregation
==============================


```{rewrite-rule}
$x$ is {ph}`AP`$_1$ and ... and $x$ is {ph}`AP`$_n$

$x$ is {ph}`AP`$_1$ and ... and {ph}`AP`$_n$
```

Examples
--------

```{rewrite-rule}
$x$ is prime and $x$ is greater than $5$

$x$ is prime and greater than $5$
```
