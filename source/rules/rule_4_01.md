Rule 4.1: "And"-AP Aggregation
==============================


```{rewrite-rule}
AP_1, ..., AP_n : <AP> = "prime", "greater than $5$"

t : <Term> = "$x$"

":t: is :AP_1: and ... and :t: is :AP_n:"

--- "And"-AP Aggregation

":t: is :AP_1: and ... and :AP_n:"
```

Examples
--------

```{rewrite-rule}
"$x$ is prime and $x$ is greater than $5$"

--- "And"-AP Aggregation

"$x$ is prime and greater than $5$"
```
