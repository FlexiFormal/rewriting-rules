Rule 3.1: Adjective Determiner Reduction
========================================

```{rewrite-rule}
{ph}`Adj` {ph}`Prep` $x$ for {ph}`Det` {ph}`NP` $x$

{ph}`Adj` {ph}`Prep` {ph}`Det` {ph}`NP` $x$
```


Examples
--------

```{rewrite-rule}
applicable in $q$ for a non-initial state $q$

applicable in a non-initial states $q$
```
