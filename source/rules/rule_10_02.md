Rule 10.2: Modal "False"-NP Reduction
=====================================


```{rewrite-rule}
it (is wrong | is false | is not the case | does not hold) that $x$ is $d$@(a | no) {ph}`NP`

$x$ is not $d$ {ph}`NP`
```


Examples
--------

```{rewrite-rule}
it does not hold that $n$ is a prime number

$n$ is not a prime number
```
