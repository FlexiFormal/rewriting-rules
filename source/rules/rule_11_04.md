Rule 11.4: AP Double Negation Elimination
=========================================


```{rewrite-rule}
$\vdash \forall \varphi.\, \neg \neg \varphi = \varphi$

$x$ is not not {ph}`AP`

$x$ is {ph}`NP`
```


Examples
--------

```{rewrite-rule}
$x$ is not not greater than $5$

$x$ is greater than $5$
```
