Rule 11.12: "Or"-Commutativity Conversion
=========================================


```{rewrite-rule}
$\vdash \forall \varphi, \psi.\, \varphi \vee \psi = \psi \vee \varphi$

{ph}`PP`$_1$ or {ph}`PP`$_2$

{ph}`PP`$_2$ or {ph}`PP`$_1$
```


Examples
--------

```{rewrite-rule}
$x$ is odd or $x$ is even

$x$ is even or $x$ is odd
```
