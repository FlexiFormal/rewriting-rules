Section 7: Equality Conversion
==============================

Provalby equal expressions can be replaced by each other:

```{rewrite-rule}
$\vdash A = A'$

$A$

$A'$
```


Examples
--------

Let $R^+$ denote the transitive closure of a relation $R$.

```{rewrite-rule}
$\vdash \bigcup_{n \in \mathbb{N}} R^n = R^+$

$\bigcup_{n \in \mathbb{N}} R^n$

$R^+$
```
