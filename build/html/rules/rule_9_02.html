Rule 9.2: Sequence Structure Expansion¶

noun : <noun> = "transition"
t : <symbolic term> = "⟨q,c,q′⟩"
x1, ..., xn : <Var> = "t1", …, "tn"
i : <Var> = "i"
noun := "A noun is a 'structure' | 'pair' | 'triple' | ... t 'with' | 'of' | 'where' ..."
sequence x1,…,xn of noun
sequence x1,…,xn of noun xi:=ti

Examples¶

transition := "A transition is a triple ⟨q,c,q′⟩ with q′∈δ(q,c)"
