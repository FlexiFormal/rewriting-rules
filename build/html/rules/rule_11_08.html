Rule 11.8: Contradiction Introduction¶

Prop : Prop = \(x\) is prime
P : Var = \(P\)
\(\vdash \forall {ph}`P`.\, {ph}`P` \wedge \neg {ph}`P` = \bot\)
Prop and it is wrong that Prop
we have a contradiction

Examples¶
