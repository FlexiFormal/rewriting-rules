Rule 4.4: "Or"-VP Aggregation
=============================


```{rewrite-rule}
${ph}`VP_1`, \ldots, {ph}`VP_n`$ : {cat}`VP`= {nl}`intersects with $y$`, {nl}`contains $z$`

{ph}`t` : {cat}`Term` = {nl}`$x$`

{ph}`t` {ph}`VP_1` or ... or {ph}`t` {ph}`VP_n`

{ph}`t` {ph}`VP_1` or ... or {ph}`VP_n`
```

Examples
--------

```{rewrite-rule}
$x$ intersects with $y$ or $x$ contains $z$

$x$ intersects with $y$ or contains $z$
```
