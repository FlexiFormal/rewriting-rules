Rule 11.9: "And"-Idempotence Reduction
======================================


```{rewrite-rule}
Prop : <Prop> = "$x$ is prime"

P : <Var> = "$P$"

|- $\forall :P:.\, :P: \wedge :P: = :P:$

":Prop: and :Prop:"

--- "And"-Idempotence Reduction

":Prop:"
```


Examples
--------

```{rewrite-rule}
|- $\forall P.\, P \wedge P = P$

"$x$ is prime and $x$ is prime"

--- "And"-Idempotence Reduction

"$x$ is prime"
```
