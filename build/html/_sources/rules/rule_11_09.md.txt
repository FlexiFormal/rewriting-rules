Rule 11.9: "And"-Idempotence Reduction
======================================


```{rewrite-rule}
{ph}`Prop` : {cat}`Prop` = {nl}`$x$ is prime`

{ph}`P` : {cat}`Var` = {nl}`$P$`

$\vdash \forall {ph}`P`.\, {ph}`P` \wedge {ph}`P` = {ph}`P`$

{ph}`Prop` and {ph}`Prop`

{ph}`Prop`
```


Examples
--------

```{rewrite-rule}
$\vdash \forall P.\, P \wedge P = P$

$x$ is prime and $x$ is prime

$x$ is prime
```
