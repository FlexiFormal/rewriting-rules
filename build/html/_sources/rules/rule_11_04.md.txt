Rule 11.4: AP Double Negation Elimination
=========================================


```{rewrite-rule}
{ph}`AP` : {cat}`AP` = {nl}`greater than $5$`

{ph}`P` : {cat}`Var` = {nl}`$P$`

{ph}`x` : {cat}`Term` = {nl}`$x$`

$\vdash \forall {ph}`P`.\, \neg \neg {ph}`P` = {ph}`P`$

{ph}`x` is not not {ph}`AP`

{ph}`x` is {ph}`AP`
```


Examples
--------

```{rewrite-rule}
$\vdash \forall P.\, \neg \neg P = P$

$x$ is not not greater than $5$

$x$ is greater than $5$
```
