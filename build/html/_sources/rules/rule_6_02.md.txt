Rule 6.2: $\iota_\subseteq$-Reduction
=====================================


```{rewrite-rule}
{ph}`a` : {cat}`Term` = {nl}`$n - 5$`

{ph}`A` : {cat}`Term` = {nl}`$\mathbb{Z}$`

$\{{ph}`a`\} \subseteq {ph}`A`$

${ph}`a` \in {ph}`A`$
```


Examples
--------

```{rewrite-rule}
$\{n - 5\} \subseteq \mathbb{Z}$

$n - 5 \in \mathbb{Z}$
```
