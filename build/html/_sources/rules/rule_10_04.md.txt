Rule 10.4: Modal "True"-VP Reduction
====================================


```{rewrite-rule}
{ph}`VP` : {cat}`VP` = {nl}`divide $5$`

{ph}`x` : {cat}`Term` = {nl}`$x$`

it (is true | is the case | holds) that {ph}`x` {ph}`VP`

{ph}`x` {ph}`VP`
```


Examples
--------

```{rewrite-rule}
it is the case that $x$ divides $5$

$x$ divides $5$
```
