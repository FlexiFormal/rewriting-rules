Rule 10.5: Modal "False"-VP Reduction
=====================================


```{rewrite-rule}
VP : <VP> = "intersect with $y$"

x : <Term> = "$x$"

"it @is wrong | is false | is not the case | does not hold@ that :x: :VP:"

":x: does not :VP:"
```


Examples
--------

```{rewrite-rule}
"it does not hold that $x$ intersects with $y$"

"$x$ does not intersect with $y$"
```
