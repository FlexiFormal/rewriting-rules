Rule 4.1: "And"-AP Aggregation
==============================


```{rewrite-rule}
${ph}`AP_1`, \ldots, {ph}`AP_n`$ : {cat}`AP` = {nl}`prime`, {nl}`greater than $5$`

{ph}`t` : {cat}`Term` = {nl}`$x$`

{ph}`t` is {ph}`AP_1` and ... and {ph}`t` is {ph}`AP_n`

{ph}`t` is {ph}`AP_1` and ... and {ph}`AP_n`
```

Examples
--------

```{rewrite-rule}
$x$ is prime and $x$ is greater than $5$

$x$ is prime and greater than $5$
```
