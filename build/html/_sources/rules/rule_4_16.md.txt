Rule 4.16: "Neither"-PP Aggregation
===================================


```{rewrite-rule}
{ph}`Prop_1`, $\ldots$, {ph}`Prop_n` : {cat}`Prop` = {nl}`$x$ is prime`, {nl}`$x$ and $y$ have a common divisor`

it (is wrong | is false | is not the case | does not hold) that {ph}`Prop_1` and ... and that {ph}`Prop_n`

neither {ph}`Prop_1` nor ... nor {ph}`Prop_n`
```

Examples
--------

```{rewrite-rule}
it is wrong that $x$ is prime and that $x$ and $y$ have a common divisor

neither $x$ is prime nor $x$ and $y$ have a common divisor
```
